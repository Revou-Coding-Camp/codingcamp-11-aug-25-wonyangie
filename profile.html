About Mergoffee

Mergoffee was born in 2024 from the passion of Temanggung youth to explore the potential of local coffee. We started by learning everything about coffee, mixing coffee grounds and experimenting with brewing methods, while learning directly from local coffee farmers and producers. Vision & Mission

Vision

To become a Temanggung local coffee brand widely recognized for its quality, sustainability, and social impact.

Mission

  • Deliver high-quality coffee through hygienic and standardized processes.
  • Collaborate fairly with local farmers and communities.
  • Educate consumers about the rich flavors of Temanggung coffee.
  • Develop innovative flavors and packaging that remain relevant.
